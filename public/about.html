About Inventory

Inventory is a fast, offline-friendly web app for everyday stock & sales management — from cafés and food trucks to retail counters and medium-size stores.

No installs
Works offline
Drag & drop tasks
CSV export
Cloud Sync (optional)

What you can do

  • Track Inventory with low/critical alerts.
  • Build a Product catalog with images.
  • Log daily COGS and see MoM/YoY trends.
  • Organize Tasks across lanes (todo → in progress → done).
  • Search everything instantly and export CSVs.

Who is it for?

Owners, managers and team members who need a simple, reliable tool: pop up shops, salons, convenience stores, coffee bars, sushi kiosks, you name it.

Why it’s different

  • Zero setup: open the link, sign in, start working.
  • Offline-first: local storage keeps you going without signal.
  • Bring-your-own cloud: optional Firebase sync, per-user.
  • Fast UI: one small page, no heavy frameworks.
