About Inventory

A lightweight, offline-friendly app that helps small and medium businesses manage stock, products, costs, and teamwork — from any device.

Why people love it

  • Fast, zero-install: runs as a PWA right in your browser.
  • Works offline: keep using it during flaky internet, sync later.
  • Role-based access: viewer, associate, manager, admin.
  • All-in-one: inventory, products, COGS, tasks, posts, and search.

Who is it for?

Independent shops, cafés, restaurants, and growing teams that need a simple system today — without servers or IT overhead.

How it works

  • Data is saved locally per signed-in user, and (optionally) mirrored to your Firebase.
  • Uploads are stored as secure data URLs (no separate image hosting required).
  • You can export CSVs at any time for audits or accounting.
