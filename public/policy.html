Privacy

  • Local first: All data is stored in your browser using LocalStorage.
  • Cloud Sync (optional): If you toggle it on, data is mirrored to your Firebase Realtime Database under your user.
  • No tracking: We don’t collect analytics by default.

Security

  • Role-based permissions for CRUD actions.
  • Firebase Auth (email/password) when you choose Cloud Sync.
