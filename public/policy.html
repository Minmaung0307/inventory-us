Policy

Data

  • Local-first: your data is stored in the browser per user account.
  • Optional Cloud Sync: if enabled, your data is mirrored to your own Firebase project.
  • Images: images are embedded as Data URLs, not uploaded to a third-party host.

Security

  • Use strong passwords. Enable Firebase security rules in production.
  • Sign out on shared devices.
