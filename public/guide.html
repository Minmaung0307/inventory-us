Quick Start

  • Login or create an account.
  • Use the sidebar to jump between Inventory, Products, COGS, Tasks.
  • Click Add to create items; hover thumbnails to preview; tap on mobile to open preview.
  • Tasks: drag between lanes (even if empty) or tap to cycle (mobile).

Tips

  • CSV export excludes images for smaller files.
  • Cloud Sync mirrors your local data to Firebase per user.
  • Search finds partial or exact words across all sections.
