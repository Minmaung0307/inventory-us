User Guide

Getting around

Use the sidebar to switch between InventoryProductsCOGSTasks and more.

Search (top of sidebar) finds items across all sections.

Adding items

  • Inventory → Add Item → fill name and optional image (supports paste/upload).
  • Products → Add Product → barcode, price, ingredients and instructions optional.
  • COGS → Add Row → the totals update automatically.

Tasks

Drag cards between lanes, or tap cards on mobile to cycle status.

Sync & Offline

Works offline by default. Turn on Cloud Sync in Settings to mirror your data to your Firebase account.
