User Guide

Getting started

  • Sign in (or use the demo admin shown on the login page).
  • Open Inventory or Products to add items and upload images.
  • Use Tasks to plan your day with drag & drop.

Tips

  • Turn on Cloud Sync in Settings to mirror your data per user to Firebase.
  • Use Export CSV for quick reports.
  • Hover image thumbnails to preview; on phone, tap to open a larger view.
